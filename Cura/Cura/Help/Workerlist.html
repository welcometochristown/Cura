Workers ››

The worker list appears on the main screen to left of the worker calendar.


With this list you can search by name or E number.



Here are the list of icons that may appear next to a worker and what they mean.



 - No core hours have been assigned to this worker.

 - This worker is a key worker and has one or more key service users.

 - This worker has been marked as being able to drive.
